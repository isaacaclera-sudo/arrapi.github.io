Mission

To be the leading organization that strengthens the Reserve Force in the Philippines by cultivating a disciplined, professional, and highly engaged community of Reservists and Reservist Administrators, who contribute to national security, social welfare, and the advancement of the rights and benefits of its members.

Vision

The Association aims to connect Reservists and Reservist Administrators across the Philippines to enhance support for the Armed Forces, uphold professionalism, and work towards the advancement of its members' rights, privileges, and well-being.
